Ordering

Our website ordering system only works with .jpg and .tif files. However we encourage our customers to use lossless compression file formats, i.e. .tif or .psd files for canvas and photo printing. Larger files can be sent to us using custom upload page. You can also use .jpg files for placing the order and upload lossless files via above link. We can also process your files in 16-bit workflow if necessary. Canvas media is more forgiving than photo papers and there isn't much visual difference above 200 dpi. We suggest to supply the files with at least 100 dpi effective resolution (not upscaled). Whenever you have any doubts we can produce proofs of image section at 1:1 scale, free of charge.
